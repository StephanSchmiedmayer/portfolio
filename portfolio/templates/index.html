Coarse-to-Fine 3D Shape Refinement

Advanced Deep Learning for Computer Vision - Visual Computing Group - TUM

Computer Vision PyTorch Transformer

Proposed a new architecture designed to enhance the geometric detail in 3D shape generation by refining sparse and noisy point clouds.

Combines the strengths of Point-Voxel Convolution (PVConv), Point Transformer, and PU-Net to refine shapes generated by Point-Voxel Diffusion (PVD) in a patch-by-patch manner.

Enriches geometric details, resulting in 3D models of higher realism and improved spatial resolution.
